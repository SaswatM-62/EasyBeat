Easy Beats is a simple yet elegant way of gaining better insight into your own body. Our system analyzes ECG reading to monitor for abnormalities while educating the user about their cardiac health.

What is EasyBeat?

How EasyBeat works?

Easy Beat uses smartwatch APIs to gather your ECG data and feeds it to a Machine Learning model. The model processes the data and generates a downloadable report. The report lets you know if there is anything abnormal with your heart. All you have to do is link your smartwatch to the website.
